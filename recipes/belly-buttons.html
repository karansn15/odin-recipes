Belly Buttons

Belly Buttons are a popular dessert dish that is easy to make and popular as a light snack to eat while enjoying time with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Pretzels (Preferrably Square)
  • Hershey's Kisses
  • m&ms

Step by Step Recipe:

  1. Place pretzels on tray
  2. Place Hershey's Kisses on top of pretzels
  3. Put tray in oven for 2-3 minutes or until chocolate has softened
  4. Put m&ms in kisses and put in fridge
  5. Wait 30 min to an hour and enjoy!
